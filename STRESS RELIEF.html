Study Break Checklist

A healthy break can reduce stress faster than endless scrolling.

  • Unclench your jaw
  • Roll your shoulders back
  • Look away from the screen
  • Take five slow breaths
  • Tell yourself one kind thing

Reset Suggestions

When stress is high, simple choices are easier than big plans.

  • High stress: close extra tabs and start with the easiest task.
  • Medium stress: use a 15-minute focus sprint with a short break.
  • Low energy: sit near light, wash your face, and restart gently.
  • Overthinking: write a messy draft first, then improve it later.

Kind Reminder

This page can support you, but it does not replace real help.

If stress starts feeling too heavy, talk to a trusted friend, faculty mentor, counselor, or campus mental health service. Asking for help is a strong step, not a weak one.
